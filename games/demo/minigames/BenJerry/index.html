TERMS & CONDITIONS: BEN & JERRY’S SPACE ADVENTURE MOBILE CONTEST (“COMPETITION”)


How to Enter
Participants stand a chance to win a pair of return flight tickets every week through Ben & Jerry’s mobile game. Participants are required to purchase any Ben & Jerry’s product in a single receipt for an opportunity to participate in the Ben & Jerry’s ‘Space Adventure’ mobile game.

Prizes
The Prizes are as follows:-
  1. Week 1: 1 pair of economy class return air tickets to Maldives (on Singapore Airlines or Silk Air).
  2. Week 2: 1 pair of economy class return air tickets to Japan (Haneda/ Narita) (on Singapore Airlines).
  3. Week 3: 1 pair of economy class return air tickets to New Zealand (Auckland) (on Singapore Airlines).
  4. Week 4: 1 pair of economy class return air tickets to Turkey (Istanbul) (on Singapore Airlines).

How to win
Participants are required to purchase any Ben & Jerry's product within the Entry Dates in a single receipt to qualify for participation. Participants can access Ben & Jerry's Space Adventure mobile game via QR code featured on in store POSM or game link on Ben & Jerry's Singapore Facebook page (http://bit.ly/BenJerryContest). Participants must accurately fill out their name (as per national ID), email, number of Ben & Jerry’s products purchased in a single receipt and receipt number and are required to fly Woody the cow through space to collect as many Ben & Jerry’s pints within the given time. Upon completion of the game, participants will have to submit their scores and attach an image of the receipt (keyed in at the start of the frame). If participants purchase more Ben & Jerry's products in a single receipt, they will get more play time (e.g. 1 product: 20 sec game, 2 products: +5 sec). Scores will be accumulated for participants who submit more entries within the same Game Week. Previous day’s scores will be announced on Ben & Jerry's Singapore Facebook page daily at 2pm and the game scores will be refreshed every Monday. There will be a new prize destination and equal chances for new participants to win every week. Receipts cannot be duplicated from previous entries.

Other Requirements
Winners must submit the original proof of purchase, for verification, prior to collection of the Prize. Game Weeks will be as set out hereinbelow (each a “Game Week”) and game scores will be refreshed at the start of each new week:
  • 1st to 10th March (ends 23:59pm) - Winner will be contacted on 11th March.
  • 11th to 17th March (ends 23:59pm) - Winner will be contacted on 18th March.

  • 25th to 31st March (ends 23:59pm) - Winner will be contacted on 1st April.
  • Scores will be accumulated within the Game Week for participants who submit receipts under the same name and email registered in game.
  • Within each Game Week, scores from the previous day will be announced daily on Ben & Jerry’s Singapore Facebook page at 2 pm so that participants can track their progress. Only the top 3 names will be announced.
